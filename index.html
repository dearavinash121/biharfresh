Welcome to Bihar Fresh

We export high-quality vegetables, pickles, and spices grown in the heart of Bihar to customers across the globe.

About Us

Bihar Fresh is committed to delivering fresh and chemical-free produce directly from farms to international buyers. We empower local farmers and promote clean, green exports.

Our Products

  • Fresh Bhindi (Okra)
  • Handmade Achar (Pickles)
  • Organic Masalas (Spices)
  • Seasonal Vegetables

Export Process

  1. Collection from Farms
  2. Cleaning & Packaging
  3. Cold Storage (if needed)
  4. Export via Air/Ship to buyer's country
